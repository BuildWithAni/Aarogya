## 🏥 The Vision
Aarogyam is a **mobile healthcare infrastructure ecosystem** designed to bring affordable, accessible, and technology-driven healthcare directly to rural and underserved populations. 

We are not just building an app; we are building the **future infrastructure of rural healthcare.**

---

## 📉 The Problem
**Healthcare in India is concentrated, but the population is distributed.**

- **65%** of India's population lives in rural or semi-rural regions.
- Healthcare infrastructure is heavily concentrated in tier-1 cities.
- Millions travel long distances, face delayed treatments, and lack diagnostics.

Traditional telemedicine fails because it assumes high connectivity and ignores the physical logistics of healthcare.

---

## 🚀 The Solution: Hospitals That Travel
Instead of patients traveling to hospitals, **Hospitals travel to patients.**

Aarogyam solves the last-mile healthcare problem by creating a complete operating ecosystem that combines:
- **Mobile Healthcare Clinics** (Fully equipped with diagnostics)
- **AI-Powered Logistics** (GPS-optimized fleet tracking)
- **Unified Telemedicine** (WebRTC-powered specialist consultations)
- **Offline-First Infrastructure** (Resilient synchronization)

---

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
- **Frontend:** Flutter (Mobile & Web)
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js
- **Database:** PostgreSQL (Scalable & Secure)
- **Cloud:** AWS (Global Infrastructure)
- **Telemedicine:** WebRTC (Low-latency video)
- **Maps:** Google Maps API (Routing & Geofencing)

**Key Advantage: Offline-First Architecture**
Rural regions have weak connectivity. Aarogyam is built to operate offline, caching all data locally and syncing automatically once reconnected.
